Toh shuru karein!!

Agar wo so rahe hain, toh kaise jaanenge? Breathing dekh ke bolte hain, ‘Haan, zinda hain!’ Plants? Wo breathe nahi karte, lekin wo green hote hain aur photosynthesis karte hain, isliye wo bhi living hote hain! Aur virus? Ye living tab hote hain jab ye kisi cell ke andar jaate hain aur multiply karte hain.

Molecular movement har living organism ka part hai, jo unko survive aur maintain karne mein help karta hai!

Agar dog ya cow chal rahe hain, ya man chill kar raha hai, toh humein samajh aata hai wo living hain.

Life Process ka matlab kya hai? Shabd ko todte hain — ‘Life’ matlab living aur ‘Processes’ matlab prakriya. Matlab, living organisms ke andar jo processes hoti hain… but wait! Processes to bohot saari hoti hain, lekin kuchhoti hain jinse hum zinda reh paate hain. Agar ye processes band ho gayi, toh survival impossible!

Toh simple shabd mein: jo processes organisms ko zinda rehne aur survive karne mein help karti hain, unko hum bolte hain Life Processes.

Life Processes

The basic and essential functions / process performed by living organism to maintain their life.
